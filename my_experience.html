FREELANCER

My experience with freelancer started because in this semester which is semester 4 we as finance student should take a subject Digital Workforce (UBM599). As the largest online freelancing platform in the world, Freelancer is a brilliant place to start if you’re a skilled professional who has decided to make their way as an independent freelancer.It has given me an opportunity that I perhaps would never have been given elsewhere when I have to bid and make a contest for winning the job that client want based on our professional skills.The platform has introduced me to wonderful clients with inspiring projects!


CITY CAMPUS BADMINTON CLUB

City Campus Badminton Club or CCBC is one of the non academic club in UiTM Kota Bharu. I joined this club just because i love badminton and want to gain more experiences handle the sports club event. Last 7th December, we has visited Akademi Badminton Malaysia at Bukit Kiara, Kuala Lumpur. I was so excited to visit the place that I really want to go whenever I watched our national athletes training at that place! Also that, I got a chance to meet our World Champions Men Doubles, Aaron Chia and Soh Wooi Yik. Since before this I just watched their tournament on screen but then I met them in real life! It was most beautiful experience for me with this CCBC club.


EKSPO SELANGKAH KE UITM

A year ago when I was in UiTM Machang, I volunteered to participate in the Ekspo Selangkah ke UiTM (ESKU) program. I joined the program because I wanted to gather experience at university and interact with people. I want to improved my communication skills when meeting people. In this program I represent the Faculty of Business and Management to deliver information to spm graduates who are looking for a course they are interested in. The program was really fun and I got the new experience that I will always remember.
